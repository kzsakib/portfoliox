Appointment Booking Voice Agent

n8n, Vapi AI

overview

Client Requirement:
The client wanted an appointment booking receptionist AI agent (voice) for their office. Appointments needed to be automatically added to Google Calendar as events, tracked in a Google Sheet, and if confirmed, trigger an email notification.
‍
‍ What I Built:
Developed a Vapi AI-powered voice agent that handled appointment booking, confirmation, and updates automatically. Integrated the system with Google Calendar to create events, updated a Google Sheet with call and appointment data, and sent automated confirmation emails. Ensured seamless AI-assisted scheduling and tracking for the client’s office operations.
